About Us

We made this game as a group of seven students from Conestoga College. We worked hard together and created something amazing. Each of us has a large set of advanced skills that we combined to make Malicious Monsters.

For seven months of hard work, we strived to create something amazing. And we did just that. During each week of production we communicated what we wanted, and put that into action.

Some of us are artists, some of us are programmers. But what we all are, is amazing for creating a game this awesome. We are all proud of the game we created, and what we were able to learn during the process.
