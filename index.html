Game Overview

Get yourself ready to escape Castle Majick in Malicious Monsters, a challenging first-person shooter filled with adrenaline and fast paced combat where you play as Nate Kane. He's an office worker who finds himself kidnapped to be a test subject for some sketchy experiments at Castle Majick, a castle led by his sinister grandfather, the leader of the "Torn" magician clan.

Prepare yourself to fight malicious monsters! Earn upgrades and blood coins by defeating monsters, multiply your wealth with the kill streak multiplier, and use them to unlock a dense variety of exciting weapons. Do you have what it takes to escape Castle Majick?

If you have any questions about the game or would like to talk to one of the developers, please don't hesitate to reach out to the Team Lead Brandon Schuitema at Bschuitema4949@conestogac.on.ca.

Game Creation

Enemies

Want to learn more about the several different and unique enemies that are in the game?

Know More

There are four different enemies in the game that are very different and unique from each other.


The 'Don't Look' enemy will only move when the player is not looking at them. They look like a set of medieval armor.


The 'Melee' enemy is a skeleton and will get up close and attack with their bony fists.


The 'Shooter' enemy is a bat and will stay back a short distance a launch flaming projectiles at the player.


The 'Exploder' enemy will slowly creep towards the player, then jump and explode in their face. They're small and bright green.


Play the game and experience these dangerous enemies first-hand!


Weapons

We have many weapons in the game. Want to know more about them?

Know More

Crush your enemies with a vast variety of weapons, from a simple branch, to flame throwers, swords, and different types of guns. Start, increase, and keep your kill streak multiplier to unlock different rewards.

In castle Majick, rest is not only a sign of weakness, but it also means death. Keep moving, shooting, and killing to increase your chances of escaping the castle.


Art

This game has a nice looking art style with some amazing looking sprites, right? Feel like learning more about them?

Know More

This game features custom art for all the different weapons and abilities in the game. And there is a lot of them. There's also a lot of well crafted models with wonderous textures, giving the game a professional and appealing appearance.

During gameplay, you'll notice all the different VFX that have been implemented for each weapon, enemy, and system, each looking better than the next.

The lighting in the game is top-tier. Play and allow yourself to immersed in it's warm glow as you shoot malicious monsters with your powerful weapons.


Systems

This game takes advatage of many well designed systems that work together beautifully. Feel like learning more about them?

Know More

In the map there are an abundance of cabinets that the player can buy new and exciting weapons from. They can be bought with blood coins which the player can earn by killing enemies and increasing their kill streak.

A HighScore system will automatically save your best time when you finish the game. This score will be saved and you can try to beat it any time you want.

Sounds, resolutions, quality, and more can all be adjusted from our settings menu. play the game according to your preferences.
