About Us

During the course of seven months, we worked as a group tirelessly to create a game that is truly spectacular. We combined our skills ranging from art to coding to create the masterpiece: Malicious Monsters.

The game was originally pitched by Brandon Schuitema as a first person shooter that took inspiration from games like Doom and Resident Evil. Through working on the project and sharing ideas, the game has advanced far past that initial concept into something we can all enjoy.

We were all from a Game Design course at Conestoga College when we created this game. We studied and learnt many skills during the creation of this game, and we had fun doing it. We hope you like the game.

Enemies

Want to learn more about the several different and unique enemies that are in the game?

Know More

Enemies were texured by Brandon and Jaspreet. Modelled by Brandon, Liam, and Karthik. Enemy coding by Vraj. There are four different enemies in the game that are very different from each other.


The 'Don't Look' enemy will only move when the player is not looking at them. They look like medieval armor statue.


The 'Melee' will get up close and attack with their bony fists.


The 'Shooter' enemy will stay back a short distance a launch flaming projectiles at the player.


The 'Exploder' enemy will slowly creep towards the player, then jump and explode in their face.
